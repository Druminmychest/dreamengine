Rocky's Dream Engine
A surrealist oracle built from collective human expression.

What it is

You share something true — a line, a fragment, an image, a secret. Anonymously, always. It enters a pool of words contributed by others who did the same.

What Rocky gives you

A poem assembled from that pool — ghosts of other people's emotions, impressions of their lives on the coalbed of human expression. The structure is ancient, drawn from the I Ching's 64 hexagrams. The content is entirely human. The result is neither predictable nor random.

What you can do with it

Think of it as an oblique strategy — a creative prompt, an unexpected mirror, something to push against. If you make things — writing, music, painting, anything — Rocky's output is raw material. React to it. Argue with it. Let it take you somewhere you wouldn't have gone alone.

Why it exists

Because something gets lost between generations. Not information — texture. The particular flavor of how a person saw the world. This is one attempt to preserve some of that, and pass it forward.

Rocky's humours

Aristotle described four aspects of the human soul — emotional, intellectual, practical, spiritual. Rocky has four as well. At the top of every page you will find a small row of colored dots. Each one is a different facet of the same presence.

The bright dot tells you where you are. The dimmer ones are other modes of Rocky's attention — hover over them to see their names, click to travel between them. The two faintest dots are not yet awake. They will be.

Rocky's Time Machine

While the Dream Engine reaches inward — into the collective subconscious, assembling something new from pooled human truth — the Time Machine reaches backward. Each day Rocky surfaces three moments from the human record: something that happened on this calendar date ten years ago, one hundred years ago, and one thousand years ago. A fourth window reaches back ten thousand years, to the edge of the Neolithic, where the record thins and Rocky speaks most carefully about what is known and what is only inferred.

The colored bar beneath each story is not decoration. It signals how much the historical record can actually be trusted — high fidelity for the recent past, honest conjecture for the deep past. Rocky does not pretend to certainty he does not have.

Rocky speaks once a day. The Time Machine does not refresh on demand. Come back tomorrow.
